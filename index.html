About Tropical Island

Tropical Island serves the tastiest Caribbean food in Houston. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes that bring bold and authentic island flavors to the table.

Our dining room is warm and relaxed, created to make you feel at home the moment you walk in. Whether you are joining us for a quick lunch or a weekend dinner with friends, Tropical Island brings the spirit of the islands to your table.

Signature Caribbean Plates

  • Mofongo with Garlic Shrimp
  • Jerk Chicken with Coconut Rice
  • Curry Goat with Plantains
  • Ropa Vieja with Black Beans
  • Dominican Sancocho
  • Puerto Rican Pernil
  • Haitian Griot and Pikliz
  • Cuban Sandwich with Yuca Fries

Our menu features favorites from the Dominican Republic, Puerto Rico, Jamaica, Haiti, and Cuba, along with rotating chef specials inspired by seasonal ingredients.

Weekly Lunch Specials

Join us Monday through Friday for affordable lunch plates that change every week. Each special includes an entre, two sides, and a drink, designed for guests who want a quick but flavorful Caribbean meal during the workday.

Caribbean Groceries

Want to make your favorite dishes at home? Tropical Island carries a curated selection of Caribbean groceries including spices, sauces, coffee, and specialty ingredients that are difficult to find in regular supermarkets.

Live Music and Atmosphere

On select evenings, enjoy live performances ranging from reggae and salsa to bachata and merengue. With music, food, and a friendly environment, Tropical Island creates the perfect island getaway right here in Houston.
